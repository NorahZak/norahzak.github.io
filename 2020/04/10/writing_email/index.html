Writing email:

Subject line

Salutation

Dear Professor xxx…

To whom it may concerned,

Introduction

name, context…

Body

purpose: action, background, ddl

clearly and detailed

template: background, progress, problem, next step…

Closing

Sincerely,

name

institude

Don’t

‘Please…’

‘Fine’

‘Date’

use intent at start of paragraph
